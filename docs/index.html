Properties

  • Noise:

    • Stationary noise (ICRA01), or
    • Modulated noise (ICRA06)

  • Rooms:

    • Anechoic (Surrey Anechoic), or
    • Reverberant (Surrey Room D)
  • Speech:
    • source: TIMIT
    • level: 65 dB RMS
  • SNR:
    • inf, or
    • high: 16 dB SNR, or
    • medium: 6 dB SNR, or
    • low: 0 dB SNR
  • Noise reduction
    • None, or
    • LogMMSE, 24dB maximum attenuation
  • WDRC
    • Attack: 5ms
    • Release: 50ms (fast), 2000ms (slow)
    • Ratio: 3:1
    • Threshold: 45dB
    • number of channels: 7
